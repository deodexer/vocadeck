Core commitment: Vocadeck does not sell, share, resell, or commercially exploit any personal data of its users. The app contains no advertising.

1. Who is responsible for data processing?

Vocadeck is an application developed and maintained by an independent developer (deodex-dev), reachable at: vocadeck.support@gmail.com.

2. Data collected

DataSourcePurpose
Email address, name, profile photo Google Sign-In Account identification

Firebase identifier (anonymous) Firebase SDK (automatic) Authentication session management

No location, contact, health, or payment data is collected.

3. Technical sub-processors (required third parties)

  • Google Firebase Authentication — Google Sign-In management. Google LLC
  • Google Cloud Firestore — encrypted synchronization of your cards across devices. Google LLC
  • MyMemory Translated.net — automatic translation (only the word/phrase entered, no identification). Translated SRL
  • Pexels and Pixabay — search for illustrations for your cards (only the word searched, no identification). Canva / Pixabay
  • Cloudflare R2 — hosting of downloadable language packs for offline use (static files only, no account data transmitted). Cloudflare, Inc.
  • Google Play Integrity / Apple App Attest (via Firebase App Check) — verifies that calls come from the official app, for anti-fraud purposes, without personal data. Google LLC / Apple Inc.
  • Device operating system voice recognition engine — used only for the "Check my pronunciation" feature; audio is processed by your device and never sent to our servers. Google / Apple depending on the device

Data sent to MyMemory, Pexels, and Pixabay contains no personal information (only the text to translate or the word searched).

4. What we do not do

  • ✅ No advertising, targeted or generic
  • ✅ No sale of data to third parties
  • ✅ No sharing of data for commercial purposes
  • ✅ No profiling, no behavioral tracking
  • ✅ No marketing analytics tools (no Google Analytics)

5. Storage and security

Your data is stored locally on your device (SQLite database) and in Google Firestore (Google servers in Europe or the United States). Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Firebase Firestore ensures that no other user can access your cards.

The detailed history of your reviews (used to fine-tune your personalized study schedule) remains exclusively local on your device and is never synced, even with a connected Google account.

6. Retention period

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. You can delete it at any time via Menu > Delete all data, which also removes it from your Google account.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

  • Right of access — your data is visible directly in the app.
  • Right of rectification — edit your cards at any time.
  • Right to erasure — two levels available in the menu: "Delete all data" removes your cards and categories without closing your account; "Delete my account" permanently deletes your account and all associated data (complete GDPR deletion).
  • Right to portability — your cards are in text format, exportable from the app settings.
  • Right to object — sign out at any time.

To exercise these rights: vocadeck.support@gmail.com

8. Minors

Vocadeck does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data about them.

9. Changes

Any significant changes will be communicated via an app update.
